2. **Short Description / Purpose**  
  - Built an interactive reporting solution focused on customer-level revenue, order behavior, and return patterns.
  - The project aims to understand customer contribution, purchasing trends, and changes in customer value over time.

3. **Technology Stack**  
  - Power BI  
  - Power Query (data transformation and cleansing)  
  - DAX (calculated measures and KPIs)  
  - Excel (source data preparation)  
  - Dimensional data modeling

4. **Data Source** 
  - Retail customer and transaction dataset
  - Includes customer demographics, orders, revenue, returns, product details, and time attributes
  - Dataset used for academic / learning purposes

5. **Features & Highlights**  

   Business Problem  
    - Customer data was fragmented, making it difficult to identify high-value customers and analyze revenue per customer and return behavior across segments.

   Goal of the Dashboard  
    - Enable customer-centric performance analysis  
    - Track revenue per customer and order trends  
    - Identify top customers and key customer segments  
    - Support stakeholder reporting with clear customer insights  

   Walkthrough of Key Visuals  
    - KPI cards showing total revenue and revenue per customer  
    - Line charts displaying revenue-per-customer trends over time  
    - Tables listing top customers by revenue and order volume  
    - Segmentation visuals for orders by income level and occupation  
    - Year-based filters for time comparison and drill-down analysis  

   Business Impact & Insights  
    - Identified a small group of customers contributing a significant share of total revenue  
    - Revealed declining revenue-per-customer trends in specific time periods  
    - Highlighted behavioral differences across income and occupation segments  
    - Improved clarity and usability of customer performance reporting
